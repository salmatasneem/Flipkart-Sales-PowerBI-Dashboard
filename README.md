# Flipkart Sales Performance & Customer Behaviour Analysis (Power BI)

## 📌 Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing e-commerce sales data inspired by Flipkart using Power BI. The goal is to transform raw sales data into meaningful insights that help understand sales performance, customer behavior, and purchasing patterns.

The dashboard provides a clear and interactive view of key business metrics to support better decision-making in areas such as marketing strategy, product performance, and customer experience.

---

## 📊 Key Insights Explored

### 1. Sales Performance Tracking
Analyzed sales trends across different product categories and time periods to identify patterns, seasonal variations, and performance changes.

### 2. Customer Behaviour & Satisfaction
Examined customer ratings to understand the relationship between customer satisfaction and product sales performance.

### 3. Payment Method Preferences
Studied customer payment choices including UPI, Credit Card, Debit Card, Net Banking, and Cash on Delivery (COD) to understand purchasing behavior.

### 4. Regional Sales Distribution
Analyzed sales across different regions to identify high-performing markets and potential growth opportunities.
